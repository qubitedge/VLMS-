Experiment 4: Elicit Literature Review
■ AIM
To use Elicit as an AI research assistant for conducting a structured literature review by submitting targeted
research questions, evaluating the relevance ranking of returned papers, extracting key findings across
multiple studies using Elicit's column-based extraction system, and synthesising a coherent evidence
summary from the results.
■ THEORY
What Is a Literature Review?
A literature review means reading many academic papers on a topic to understand what researchers have already
discovered. Traditionally this takes days or weeks. Elicit automates the search and extracts key findings for you.
